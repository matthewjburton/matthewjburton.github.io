Matthew Burton

graphic designer game developer programmer

2007

My education began traditionally, but as I entered third grade I made the important decision to participate in the Central Based Gifted program. This meant that I would attend a school for accelerated learning. This was a difficult transition, as I had to leave the comfort of my friends to pursue a greater education, but in retrospect this was one of the best decisions I have ever made.

2012

In middle school I again opted in for the CBG program surrounding myself with intelligent, driven, and inspiring individuals.

2016

When it came time to select my highschool I chose to apply for the specialty center at the Mathematics and Science highschool at Clover Hill. Again I was starting over socially, but I had dealt with this challenge twice before, so it was a simple adjustment to acclimate to my new friends.

2020

Finally, I enrolled at High Point University, where for the first time, I opted against their honors program in favor of spending more time engaged with other students and organizations. During my freshman year I was inducted into Alpha Lambda Delta Honors Society for students who achieved a 3.5 GPA during their first year in college. Later, during my junior year I was inducted into the National Society of Leadership and Sucess for my academic success and leadership roles. My educational journey has been rigorous, but rewarding which fostered my love for learning, and established a disciplined work ethic for delivering high quality solutions. I look forward to completing my B.S. in Computer Science at HPU and hope to continue learning even after graduation.
