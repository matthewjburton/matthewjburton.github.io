Programming

Programming has been an interest and focus of mine since I was young. Solving problems systematically has always appealed to my logical side, and furthered my curiosity for the field. I started learning Javascript, Python, and HTML on Codecademy in middle school, and continued to study Java in highschool. Now pursuing my B.S. in Computer Science, I have gained a wealth of knowledge in C++, Python, MySQL, data structures, computer systems, web design, and app development. Recently, I joined the Computer Science Society at High Point University, created my GitHub account, and started training on LeetCode. I look forward to what my coding future holds for me, and would love to do more with AI, deep learning, and research.

Game Development

Making games has been an effective way to strengthen my interactive design skills. I've experimented with a vast range of platforms including Scratch, Minecraft, GameMaker, and Unity. Unity stuck with me because of the flexibile nature of implementing features through C#, and for simultaneously improving my programming skills. I tried learning Unity as an ambitious, inexperienced kid, but had little success. Ultimately, I overcame the challenges I faced as a kid by attending the George Mason Envision Experience which kickstarted my ability to create games on my own. I followed up those camps with my first solo project Endless Breakout, which I revisited in 2022 to complete.

Graphic Design

Throughout high school I studied graphic design and refined my ability to produce effective visual solutions. I began offering my services on Instagram, but also branched into other service providers like 99Designs and Fiverr. I developed a portfolio of cover art, logos, brands, geofilters, apparel designs, and more. I found success in the services I provided, and many clients returned for further art after our first interaction. Freelance and client focused work bolstered my interpersonal business skills, and allowed me to exhibit the compassionate business model I value. I learned a lot about understanding a client's needs, and experimenting with multiple solutions to reach a suitable product.
